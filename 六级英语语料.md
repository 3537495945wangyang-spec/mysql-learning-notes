### **一、常见逻辑关系词**

#### （一）表示原因

because, since

because of =  due to = owing to 

#### （二）表示结果

so =  therefore =  as a consequence

#### （三）表示并列

and =  similarly ＝ in the meanwhile

#### （四）表示转折

but =  on the contrary

#### （五）表示递进

 also ＝  in addition =furthermore 

#### （六）表示引出新对象

1. about = regarding 
    
2. in terms of
    
3. when it comes to...
    

#### （七）表示总结

 substantially

It is ... that ...
